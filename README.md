# Hi, I’m Poulomi 👋

I’m a Senior Data Analyst based in the U.S., with a background that started in data engineering and grew into analytics, strategy, and storytelling.

Before moving into analytics, I worked as a Data Engineer at HP, where I built scalable ETL pipelines, automated workflows, and worked with large enterprise data systems. That engineering foundation still shapes how I approach analytics today: I care not just about the final dashboard or insight, but also about whether the data is clean, reliable, scalable, and actually useful.

I hold a Master’s degree in Data Analytics, and my experience spans startups, big tech, and government. That mix has made me adaptable. I’ve worked in environments with access to advanced tools and large-scale systems, and I’ve also worked in resource-constrained settings where creativity, problem-solving, and practical solutions matter just as much as technical skill.

Currently, I work as a Senior Data Analyst in public sector, where I use Python, SQL, Power BI, and large public-sector datasets to support policy, operations, reporting, and strategic decision-making.

I’m especially interested in projects that sit at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, automation, and real-world impact.

## What I bring

- Strong foundation in data engineering, ETL, and pipeline development
- Hands-on experience with Python, SQL, Power BI, Spark, Databricks, AWS, and Alteryx
- Experience working with large datasets, messy real-world data, and stakeholder-driven analysis
- Ability to turn complex data into clear insights, reports, dashboards, and decisions
- Adaptability across startup, big tech, academic, and government environments

## What I’m building here

This GitHub is a space for projects that reflect how I think through data problems, from cleaning and engineering data pipelines to building analytics workflows, dashboards, and insight-driven stories.

I’m always learning, building, and looking for better ways to make data useful.
